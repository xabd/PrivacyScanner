Privacy Policy for PrivacyScanner

PrivacyScanner is an Android app that scans installed apps on your device and highlights potential privacy risks by analyzing their permissions. It helps users understand which apps may access sensitive information.

Data Collection

PrivacyScanner does not collect any personal data. The app only analyzes permissions of installed applications locally on your device. All processing happens on your device and no data is sent externally.

Data Sharing

We do not share any information collected by the app with third parties. No personal or device data leaves your device during normal app usage.
